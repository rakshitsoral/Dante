  <p>When I joined Kitecyber in October 2024 as Marketing and Demand Generation Specialist, I stepped into a fascinating challenge: a cybersecurity startup with cutting-edge AI technology‚Äîan AI security copilot for endpoints and network‚Äîbut no marketing function to speak of.</p>
  
  <p>Kitecyber had built a robust product that could compete with established players in the cybersecurity space. However, they faced critical gaps: unclear market positioning, undefined product categories, inconsistent messaging across channels, and zero demand generation infrastructure. The founders knew they had built something valuable, but the market didn't know Kitecyber existed.</p>

This is the story of building marketing infrastructure that turned a hidden gem into a visible contender.</p>
